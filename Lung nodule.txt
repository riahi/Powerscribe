Please refer to below summary of Fleischner criteria recommendations for follow-up of incidental CT nodules.

(H MacMahon, Guidelines for management of small pulmonary nodules detected on CT scans: A statement from the Fleischner Society, Radiology 237: 395-400 2005.)
Low Risk Patient (Minimal or no smoking or other known risk factors for malignancy): Initial follow-up CT at 12 months; if unchanged, no further follow-up. 
High Risk Patient (History of smoking or other known risk factors): Initial follow-up CT at 6-12 months then at 18-24 months if no change.
